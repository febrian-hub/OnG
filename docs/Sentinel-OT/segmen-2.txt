Sentinel-OT: Segmen 2 - Network, SCADA & DCS (Ultimate Open-Source Plan)
I. OPEN-SOURCE FRAMEWORK & TOOLS (The Engine)
• Network Emulation: Containerlab & GNS3 (Simulasi switch/router industri & firewall).

• Traffic Analysis & IDS: Zeek (Bro) & Suricata (Deep Packet Inspection & Network Security Monitoring).

• SCADA/HMI: ScadaBR / OSHMI (Visualisasi kontrol & monitoring).

• DCS Orchestration: Tango Controls (Simulasi sistem kontrol terdistribusi).

• Protocol Stacks: Opendnp3 (DNP3), Libiec61850 (IEC 61850), PyModbus (Modbus).

• Honeypot: Conpot (ICS Honeypot).

• SIEM & Visualization: ELK Stack (Elasticsearch, Logstash, Kibana) & Grafana.

• Packet Manipulation: Scapy & TShark.

II. 25 MODUL STRATEGIS (Ultimate Networking)
1. Multi-Protocol DPI: Bedah paket Modbus, S7, DNP3, IEC 104, & BACnet via Zeek.

2. Alarm Suppression Detector: Deteksi pembungkaman alarm di sisi SCADA.

3. HMI Integrity & Web-Attack Guard: Monitoring XSS/Injection pada portal HMI.

4. Engineering Workstation (EWS) Audit: Tracking aktivitas akses logic DCS.

5. DCS Redundancy Consistency: Monitoring sinkronisasi controller primary/secondary.

6. False Data Injection Detector: Verifikasi konsistensi nilai HMI vs Register PLC.

7. Lateral Movement Tracer: Visualisasi perpindahan trafik antar zona sel produksi.

8. Digital Twin Process Sync: Animasi SVG mesin yang sinkron dengan data real-time.

9. DNP3 Secure Auth Audit: Monitoring keamanan protokol WAN SCADA.

10. Historian Log Integrity: Perlindungan database historis dari modifikasi ilegal.

11. Forbidden Path Detection: Alert akses langsung Level 2 ke Level 0.

12. Zoning & Conduit Auditor: Verifikasi segmentasi jaringan (IEC 62443).

13. Industrial Honeypot Manager: Monitoring deteksi serangan pada Conpot.

14. Network Resilience Monitor: Deteksi Broadcast Storm & Latency Spike.

15. Automated PCAP Forensics: Perekaman otomatis saat insiden terdeteksi.

16. Industrial Firewall Auditor: Audit ruleset pada virtual firewall (pfsense/vyos).

17. OT DMZ Boundary Monitor: Pengawasan trafik IT-OT bridge.

18. Redundancy Protocol Integrity: Monitoring PRP/HSR (Parallel Redundancy).

19. Rogue AP & Wireless Detector: Deteksi Wi-Fi liar di area pabrik (Kismet).

20. Private 5G/IIoT Security: Monitoring trafik nirkabel industri (MQTT/CoAP).

21. Port Security & SNMP Auditor: Monitoring status fisik port switch industri.

22. Industrial DNS/NTP Protector: Deteksi spoofing pada layanan waktu & nama.

23. Micro-segmentation Validator: Verifikasi isolasi antar sel manufaktur.

24. Zero Trust OT Broker: Implementasi identitas perangkat sebelum komunikasi.

25. Network Vulnerability Scanner: Scanning kerentanan pada network devices (OpenVAS).
