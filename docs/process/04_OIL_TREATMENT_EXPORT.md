### e. Oil Export Pumps

- Pompa berkapasitas besar (centrifugal / PD)
- Mengirim minyak ke:
  - Pipeline
  - Floating Storage (FSO)
  - Shuttle tanker
- Biasanya dilengkapi:
  - Standby pump
  - Minimum flow recycle
